## Starry
> Thanks for visiting! I hope you can find some contentment from staring into the sky.<br/><br/>I do not own any of the music featured in this project. Please support the artists of the wonderful tracks featured here. These are some of my favourite songs to listen to when pondering amongst the stars.

#### Hello Stephen Hawking - Holland Patent Public Library
> This lovely track was the inspiration for this project in the first place.<br/>Check out the [Holland Patent Public Library Site](https://www.hollandpatentpubliclibrary.com/ "Great things ahead.").<br/>Check out the [Holland Patent Public Library SoundCloud](https://soundcloud.com/user-794865054 "SoundCloud").

#### The Nutcracker Pas de Deux (Slowed) - Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky
> This incredible composer's works are now in the public domain. I encourage you to attend live performances of his timeless achievements, or at the very least, educate yourself on his life and career. It's quite a remarkable [story](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pyotr_Ilyich_Tchaikovsky/ "Wikipedia").

#### Moon 2 (Fjord) - Minecraft 1.0.16.05 OST
> I must apologize, I could not find a reliable and definitive answer as to who composed this piece featured in an old Minecraft version, but I did go down quite the rabbit hole of long forgotten digital efforts and lore. Please do yourself a favour and stumble down this captivating trail as I did.
